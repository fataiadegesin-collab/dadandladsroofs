What We Do

Two Specialisations.
One Standard of Excellence.

We focus on what we do best — so every roof we touch meets the same uncompromising bar.

01
Wooden Roof Framework & Trusses

We design and install precision-engineered wooden roof trusses — king post, queen post, fink, and hipped configurations — sized and spaced for your exact span and load. Every timber treated, graded, and fitted by trained craftsmen.

02
Roofing Sheet Installation

From long-span aluminium and stone-coated steel to corrugated iron, we supply and install roofing sheets with watertight precision. Proper lapping, sealed ridges, and correct pitch — every time, on every project size.

03
Full Roofing Contracts

End-to-end roofing contracts for residential, commercial, estate, and industrial structures across Lagos, Ogun, and Oyo. Single point of accountability from measurement to handover.
